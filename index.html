About Me

              From a young age, I've been captivated by the world of design, scrutinizing and dissecting the intricacies of the built environment. My innate creative and analytical disposition pulled me towards my calling – the realm of Architecture and Design.

My journey as an architect laid the foundation for my unique approach to User Interface and User Experience (UI/UX) design. Through five years of academic study and a year in practice, I developed problem-solving, critical thinking, and creative skills. I became a quality-driven professional, skilled in handling multifaceted design demands. Interpersonal skills, leadership, and effective communication became second nature.

My education and experiences seamlessly transitioned me into the digital design field. Passion for design and technology led me to Canada, where I pursued a post-graduate program in Interactive Media Management at Centennial College, Toronto.

In summary, my journey as a designer is a fusion of my architectural roots, my passion for design, and my unwavering commitment to enhancing user experiences in the digital world. Design, to me, is not just about aesthetics; it's about using the principles of design to solve real-world problems and create meaningful, lasting impact. This is my "why" – the driving force behind my pursuit of excellence in the ever-expanding realm of interactive media.
